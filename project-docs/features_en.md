# Project Features

The system includes the following modules:

## 1. Dashboard
Key business metrics displayed via charts and summary statistics.

## 2. POS (Point of Sale)
Interface for selling products to customers efficiently.

## 3. Inventory
Control over stock levels of products and raw materials. Alerts for low stock.

## 4. Recipes (BOM)
Calculation of product cost prices based on raw material usage.

## 5. HR & Payroll
Employee attendance tracking and salary calculation based on sales and penalties.

## 6. Accounting (Expenses & Income)
Tracking all business expenses and calculating Net Profit (P&L).

## 7. Daily Reports
Summary of daily business operations.
